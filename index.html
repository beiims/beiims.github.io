About BOSSCAT

BOSSCAT is an independent game development studio.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Our goal is to create games that are not only tons of fun, but are rewarding in new and unique ways. Our games are free to play, so check them out below!

SUMO! King of the hill - ON ICE!



Okay, so it doesn't actually have any sumo wrestling. BUT, can you be the last standing on the ever shrinking ice? As the heat turns up the ring starts to melt and fall away, making it harder and harder to stay on! Be careful bumping in to each other, you might get blasted! Choose a class, collect power-ups, and earn gems by sliding into first place!

It's SNOW DAY! School's out and you and your friends are about to have the craziest snowball fight you can imagine.



Ball up some snow and throw it as fast as your blocky hands can manage. Snow Day is a no-holds-barred arcade style shooter where you compete with your friends to be the most unrelenting snowballer imaginable. Slide around cover, charge up massive powerballs icing whoever is unfortunate enough to be in the way, and collect power-ups that spawn randomly across the map. What are you waiting for?
